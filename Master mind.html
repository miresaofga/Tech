Oromo Christian church of Philadelphia

Welcome to Oromo christian Church of Philadelphia

I am not ashamed of the Gospel, for it is the power of God for salivation to everyone who believes. Roman 1:16 We are a vibrant and close-knit community of believers who gather to worship, learn, and grow together. Our mission is to preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ and share His love with everyone. Whether you’re a long-time member or a first-time visitor, you’ll find a warm and welcoming atmosphere here at Rehoboth. Join us as we journey in faith, fellowship, and service. Our church family is diverse, representing various backgrounds, cultures, and walks of life. Here, you’ll find a place to worship, learn, and grow together.

Worship Services

Join us in vibrant worship and the Word of God — conducted in Afaan Oromo, allowing connection with God in your heart language.

Our Mision

To preach the full Gospel of Jesus Christ and make disciples, producing godly and productive citizens. The Bible is our foundation of success in spirit, soul, and body.

Bible study

Dive deep into God's Word with our weekly sessions. Learn, discuss, and grow spiritually with us.

Our Ministry.

  • Children’s Ministry: Nurturing young hearts in Christ’s love.
  • Youth Ministry: Empowering the next generation to live by faith.
  • Women’s Fellowship: Uplifting and connecting women through faith.
  • Men’s Brotherhood: Supporting godly leadership among men.
  • Prayer & Intercession: Seeking God’s guidance through prayer.
  • Community Outreach: Showing Christ’s love through service.
